About This Illusion
This infinite hallway uses parallax scrolling and perspective distortion to create the illusion of endless depth. Objects closer to you move faster than those further away, simulating real-world depth perception.
Controls
• Adjust the Speed slider to control how fast you move through the hallway
• Adjust the Depth slider to change the perceived depth of the hallway
• The hallway will continue looping infinitely
